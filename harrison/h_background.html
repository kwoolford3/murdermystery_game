Getting to know Harrison

Innocent Bear Cub...or a ferocious killer?

Personal Background

Harrison is a MS HCI-ID student. He has a background in physics, math, and computer science, and in the past has worked on developing interactive technologies of his own creation such as personality robots, window displays, and immersive reality devices. His current research with the lab centers around interactive toys, wearable technology, and tools for physical computing education. He holds a Graduate Teaching Assistantship.

Psychological Assesment

are Harrison is independent enough to have a sense of his own innate strengths as well as weaknesses. He is purposeful and decisive. Throughout his lifetime, he's shown to have the tendency to work and work. As is the case with most workaholics, he has the potential to grow into a responsible leaders and excellent organizer. Nevertheless, his detached and self-focused characteristics sometimes cause him to underestimate the gravity of his errors

Socialization Assesment

Harrison is humble and gentle, yet fiercely protective of his loved ones. He is very ambitious and likes to be on top, the very top, socially, financially, and free from idealistic dreams and foolish illusions. He also has a need to show accomplishment, while being respected. When upsetted, Harrison tends to draw back and grows silent and gloomy.
